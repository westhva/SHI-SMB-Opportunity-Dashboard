Template 5: IT/Procurement Focus

Lead with shadow IT and efficiency angle

Subject: Question about design/content tool visibility

Hi [IT/Procurement Contact],

Quick question - do you have visibility into what design or content creation tools your teams are using across the organization?

I ask because we're working with several clients who discovered they had shadow IT in this area - scattered Canva accounts on credit cards, individual Adobe subscriptions, free tools that pose data concerns. It usually flies under procurement's radar until someone actually looks.

We've been working with Canva to help consolidate this category. From an IT/procurement perspective, the benefits are pretty straightforward:

  • SSO/SCIM integration - not managing another credential set
  • Centralized billing - instead of dozens of credit card subscriptions
  • Usage visibility - admin controls to actually see who's using what
  • Adobe optimization - most people with Creative Cloud only need basic design capability

Example: One client discovered 73 individual Canva Pro subscriptions they didn't know existed. Consolidated to Enterprise, got better controls, actually reduced cost. Another client cut their Adobe footprint by 40% by moving non-designers to Canva.

Not saying this is definitely relevant for [Organization Name], but would you be open to a 30-minute call with Canva? They can walk through the admin/procurement side and we can see if there's an efficiency play here.

Low commitment - worst case you spend 30 minutes. Best case you get ahead of shadow IT and optimize some software spend.

Let me know if you're open to it.

Best,
[Your Name]
