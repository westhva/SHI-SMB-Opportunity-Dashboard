Template 1: Direct & Data-Driven

Best for initial outreach when you have usage data

Subject: This may surprise you, summary of your Canva usage

Hi [relevant key contact],

Hope you're doing well.

Wanted to let you know that we've recently brought on Canva as a new software vendor.

Canva has a process where I can request high level usage insights for my customers and I've proactively asked for your summary.

Key finding: There are [X] number of unique users using Canva at [Organisation name]

Most of these users will likely be on individual licences, which present the following shortfalls/risks:

  • Design content is owned by individuals, with no ability to retain IP if employees depart
  • No single sign on
  • Licence is tied to a specific user, with no ability to reallocate licences
  • No centralised billing, credit card purchasing only
  • No ability to control AI usage and opt users out of model training

To address these challenges, we're providing Canva Enterprise to our customers which gives full control over all of the above - and much more.

If you're open to it, I'd like to set up a meeting to review your current usage and explore how Canva Enterprise might better align to your IT governance/compliance standards.

I'd also bring along a Canva specialist to help with any questions you might have.

Following the meeting, Canva can also provide the actual user email IDs and version types to give you a full picture.

Please let me know if any of these dates/times could work for you?

[Time 1]
[Time 2]
[Time 3]

Best regards,
[Your Name]

Template 2: Direct & Urgent

More assertive, emphasizes risk and IP ownership

Subject: Your Canva Account

Hi [first_name],

Quite a few of your team members ([X users] to be exact) are using Canva to design, collaborate on projects, and to drive campaigns.

This is happening outside of any Enterprise Service Agreement. That means all of that content, IP, and brand work? It's owned by the individual users, not [Account.name].

Most people don't realize Canva offers a corporate-grade platform designed not just to protect the business and its IP, but to empower users to work, collaborate, and create on-brand content at scale.

I know you're busy running the show. If you'd rather connect me with someone who oversees internal tools, brand, or comms tech, I'd be happy to help consolidate the rogue accounts under one [company] account.

Best,
[Your Name]

Template 3: Consultative & Helpful

Softer approach, positions you as advisor

Subject: Quick question about your team's Canva usage

Hi [Customer Name],

I wanted to reach out because I noticed [Organization Name] is using Canva across your team.

As your SHI rep, I work closely with Canva to help organizations like yours get more value from tools you're already using. Based on your current usage, it looks like you might benefit from their Enterprise plan - better admin controls, centralized billing, brand management, and usually better pricing than individual subscriptions.

I'd love to connect you with our Canva team for a quick 30-minute call. They can review your current usage and show you what an Enterprise setup would look like for [Organization Name]. No obligation - just want to make sure you're getting the most value.

Are you open to a brief intro call in the next week or two? I'll join to help facilitate.

Let me know what works for you.

Best,
[Rep Name]
SHI
