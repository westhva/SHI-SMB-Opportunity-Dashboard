FOLLOW THROUGH

Most of the customers I'm sharing this information with are getting surprised by the number of people using Canva within their organisation. The main reason I'm proactively getting this information for my customers is that the people using Canva at work are likely using an individual licence, which presents quite a few risks to an organisation of your size. The big 3 things that I like to make my customers aware of are as follows:

Firstly, if a user is licensed with the individual version, the design content is owned by the user, not the company. There's also no ability to retain design IP if an employee departs.

Secondly, individual licences can only be paid for by credit card and the licence is always tied to that user, with no ability to transfer that licence. If a user leaves the organisation, they take their software with them.

And lastly, it doesn't offer Single Sign On or give you the ability to control important security items such as AI usage and model training.
